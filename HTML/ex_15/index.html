WYMAN: Bombers put it all together in dominant win over Redblacks

OTTAWA — Many of us have been waiting to see just what the Winnipeg Blue Bombers might look like when they put everything together in a game.

We got a pretty good view of that on Friday night at TD Place, when the Bombers dominated in most areas on the way to a 29-14 win over the Ottawa Redblacks.

The offence moved the football and got into the end zone, the defence took the ball away and kept the Redblacks out of the end zone and a special teams play just might have saved the day after a short run of mistakes gave Ottawa life.
